SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great easy recipe for to make simple choclate cakes. It uses very common ingredients found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana.

EQUIPMENT

  • Cake tin or muffin tray
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer
  • Cooling rack
  • Fork
  • Weighing scales
  • Smaller bowls, to hold ingrediants

MIXING

  • Get all ingredients together and cut up
  • Put in bowl gradually, or until it looks nice and mixed
  • Make sure it's all mixed properly

COOKING

Make sure that the oven has been preheated to around 400F so that it can be qiuckly baked. After it is done preheating, pour the mixture into a cake pan, and put the cake in for around 20-30 minutes, depending on how "done" you want your cake to be
